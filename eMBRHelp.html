eMBRHelper: Decision support for biodegradation

Welcome to eMBRHelper, the decision support resource.
This tool combines information from a biodegradation network constructed from reactions in UMBBD* and KEGG* with other relevant biological and chemical information, and highlights possible biodegradation routes/paths for named contaminants. It also provides compound- and reaction-specific information to enable informed decisions for improved bioremediation.
